BEST BEACHES IN PHILIPPINES

  • Boracay Beach

    Known for its powdery white sand and crystal-clear waters, Boracay is the epitome of a tropical paradise.

  • El Nido Beach

    A haven for divers and beach lovers alike, El Nido is renowned for its limestone cliffs and vibrant marine life.

  • Palawan Beach

    Often considered the best island in the world, Palawan offers stunning seascapes and hidden lagoons.

  • Siargao Beach

    The surfing capital of the Philippines, Siargao is famous for its laid-back vibe and impressive waves.

  • Bohol Beach

    Home to the Chocolate Hills and beautiful beaches, Bohol is a diverse destination with much to offer.

  • Cebu Beach

    Known for its historical significance and stunning beaches, Cebu is a blend of culture and natural beauty.

  • Coron Beach

    Coron: A diver's paradise, Coron is famous for its underwater shipwrecks and pristine coral reefs.

  • Pagudpud Beach

    Often referred to as the "Boracay of the North," Pagudpud offers unspoiled beaches and majestic views.

  • Camiguin Beach

    A volcanic island with hot springs, waterfalls, and stunning beaches, Camiguin is an island of adventures.

  • Samal Beach

    Just off the coast of Davao, Samal Island boasts pristine beaches and a rich marine ecosystem.

  • Kalanggaman Island

    Known for its long stretch of sandbar, Kalanggaman is a hidden gem in Leyte.

  • Anawangin Beach

    A crescent-shaped beach with a backdrop of pine trees, Anawangin Cove is perfect for camping and beachcombing.

  • Panglao Island

    Part of the Bohol province, Panglao is famous for its stunning white-sand beaches and world-class diving spots.

  • Malapascua Island

    Known for its thresher sharks, Malapascua offers an unforgettable underwater experience.

  • Cagbalete Island

    A serene island in Quezon Province, Cagbalete is perfect for those seeking peace and quiet in a natural setting.
